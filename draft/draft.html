Welcome To Auerfarm

Photo of me

Inspiring children and adults to engage in, learn about, and enjoy agriculture, science, and the natural environment. At Auerfarm, we cultivate a passion for agriculture, science, and the natural world through immersive educational experiences. Our hands-on programs inspire children and adults alike to connect with the land, understand sustainable practices, and envision a future where both people and the environment flourish. Working alongside local schools, families, and community partners, we create a vibrant ecosystem of learning that extends far beyond our 120-acre farm. From our fields and gardens to our barns and forests, every corner of Auerfarm serves as a living classroom, nurturing curiosity and fostering environmental stewardship..

Spring vacation camp

Spend your Spring vacation with us! Have some time off from school? What better way to spend it than at the farm! Play with farm animals, explore the play forest, and learn how the farm prepares for spring and summer! At Auercamp, our mission is to inspire kind, confident, and resilient campers to connect to nature and the environment. Our days are spent outside climbing trees, building forts, engaging with farm animals, fishing in the stream, hiking in the woods, playing sports, cooking with farm produce, and so much more. Using a S.T.E.M. learning framework we structure games and activities that teach, inspire, and excite campers to be stewards of the environment. Each day campers go home dirty, tired, and happy.


Annual Party

The Annual Party in Plaid fundraiser is Auerfarm’s largest fundraising effort of the year. An elegant four-course dinner followed by a live auction with live music brings together friends of Auerfarm to raise money in support of our educational programming and agricultural practices.
